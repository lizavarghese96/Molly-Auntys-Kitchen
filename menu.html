The Menu

Four types of pothichoru. All made fresh every morning. All packed in banana leaf where possible.

What is pothichoru?

Pothichoru is a traditional Kerala meal packed in banana leaf — rice with a combination of curries, pickles, and sides. The banana leaf subtly flavours the rice and keeps it warm. It is comfort food, it is nostalgic, and there is nothing quite like it made at home.

Pricing principles

Prices must cover ingredients, gas, packaging, and delivery — and still leave a fair profit. We will calculate the exact cost per portion during the April test batch and price from there. Never underprice just to get orders. Underpricing attracts the wrong customers and burns aunty out.

Packaging
Preferred

Banana leaf

Authentic, looks beautiful, great for Instagram content. The leaf flavours the rice slightly. Source locally from a nearby market.

Backup

Eco-friendly box

For when banana leaf is unavailable. Use a sturdy, leak-proof box. Cost to be included in the order price.

Important note on fish orders

Fish only for nearby customers in summer

Bangalore in May is very hot. Fish spoils faster than the other options. During the first months, only accept fish orders from customers in Malleshpalya and Kaggadasapura — within 15–20 minutes delivery. No fish for far addresses until we have better packaging sorted.

What goes into each pack — aunty decides

The exact sides, curries, and accompaniments inside each pothichoru are fully aunty's decision. She is the cook. She knows what tastes best and what is practical to prepare in bulk. We trust her judgement entirely on the food side.
