What to track every week

Total orders taken  ·  Write it down every day
Total revenue collected  ·  Sum of all UPI payments received
Total ingredients spent  ·  Keep every receipt or note every purchase
Gas share  ·  Estimate based on cylinder usage
Packaging cost  ·  Note cost of leaves or boxes bought
Net profit = Revenue − all costs
Split equally every Sunday

Payment from customers

UPI only — collected before delivery

No cash. No pay on delivery. This avoids awkward situations and means the money is in hand before we spend on ingredients or gas that day. Google Pay or PhonePe both work fine.

If an order is cancelled

If a customer cancels after we have already cooked — no refund. The food is already made. If we cancel on the customer — full refund immediately, no argument. Our reliability is our reputation.
